Founded in 2010, Agha Air Travel began with a singular mission: make premium travel accessible. What started as a small agency in Karachi has evolved into a next-generation travel operating system.

Over 15 years, we've connected 10,000+ travelers to 500+ destinations through 150+ airline partnerships. From Umrah pilgrimages to corporate travel — every journey is powered by our intelligent systems.

Three pillars drive us: unbeatable pricing algorithms, exceptional human service, and deep industry expertise built over decades.

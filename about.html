About Agha Air Travel

Our Story

Built on Trust,
Powered by Passion

Founded in 2010, Agha Air Travel began with a simple mission: make premium travel accessible to everyone. What started as a small agency in Karachi has grown into one of Pakistan's most trusted travel companies.

Over the past 15 years, we've helped over 10,000 travelers reach their destinations safely and comfortably. From family vacations to corporate travel, from Umrah pilgrimages to group tours — we handle it all with care and professionalism.

Our success is built on three pillars: unbeatable prices, exceptional service, and deep industry expertise. Every trip we plan is a testament to our commitment to excellence.
